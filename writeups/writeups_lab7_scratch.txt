Non-verbal predicates in Matsigenka take on slightly different forms for the following categories:

NPs: ----------------------------------------

1) COP ("the cat is the dog" = cat COP dog)

Example sentence:
	i-nti surari 
	3mS-COP male 
	He is male

PPs: ----------------------------------------

2) =LOC ("the cat is in the park" = cat COP park=LOC)
3) COP.LOC ("the cat walks where the dog is" = cat walk dog-COP.LOC)

+ can be both modifier and complement (note: we trying to figure out how to handle this, will ask in OH tmr)
